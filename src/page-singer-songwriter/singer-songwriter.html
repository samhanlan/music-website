background

British Berlin-based singer-songwriter.

Since early years spent in the small-town basement studio writing and performing with my first band, that frenetic energy has given way to a softer-edged acoustic direction.

After moving to Berlin in 2015, the combination of time and good company has helped me explore my solo material further than I expected. Now here I am about to release my debut EP Sharp Relief, a record I'm proud to finally share.
