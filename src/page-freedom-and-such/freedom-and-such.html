ABOUT

When I was 14 or 15, I was hit by a car, not badly, but enough to pay for a few days off school. My leg was nicely bruised so I sat in the cellar with Audacity, a mic, and some household hardware that I recorded hitting with other domestic items.

I still have that shit in mind when I make a track now, usually based around a messy phone recording of something I'll have heard somewhere. The Windbreaker EP is the result of a few years of these tracks getting leaner and tighter.

For now I'm back in the studio, playing shows as hard and as often as possible, while working on side projects all upside your head.
